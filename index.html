What exactly is Cookiff?

Cookiff is a young start-up created in November 2021 by a student (Antoine Laval) on the Hec Paris campus. Passionate about pastry and inspired by the founders of Michel et Augustin, he launched his dream: to create cookies that resemble him in a perspective that respects the environment and the workers of the planet.

Our products

Cookiff, as its name indicates, specializes in the making of artisanal and original cookies. We want to combine an exquisite taste with the uniqueness of a product to discover. Thus, we try in our kitchen-lab different kinds of cookies aiming to surprise you! Our last innovation is the mango cookie. This product combines the freshness of the mango with the consistency and the power in mouth of the conquis, what to surprise you and satisfy you!

Our mission

The company's mission is to respect the dignity of workers and to reduce its carbon footprint as much as possible. The company has a charter in which it undertakes not to seek profitability (in this respect it undertakes not to be listed on the stock exchange).
